Straightforward answers about the service area, licensing, what gets done on a typical job, and how to get an estimate. Don't see your question here? Call Stefano at 617-529-1019.

What areas does Sestito Electrical serve?

+

Sestito Electrical is based in Middleton, Massachusetts and serves the entire North Shore of Massachusetts, including Topsfield, Boxford, Hamilton, Wenham, Ipswich, Manchester-by-the-Sea, Beverly, Danvers, Andover, North Andover, Lynnfield, North Reading, Marblehead, Peabody, and Gloucester. For larger projects, Stefano also travels throughout Massachusetts, New Hampshire, and Rhode Island.

Is Sestito Electrical licensed and insured in Massachusetts?

+

Yes. Stefano Sestito holds a Massachusetts Master Electrician license and carries full liability insurance and a surety bond. Every job is pulled under permit when code requires it, and all inspection costs are included up front in the written estimate.

Who actually does the work on a Sestito Electrical job?

+

Stefano Sestito personally leads every project. He is the owner-operator, not a salesman who hands jobs off to a crew. With 20 years of IBEW Local 103 union experience, he is on site for the key phases of every install and is accountable from estimate through final inspection.

Does Sestito Electrical install EV chargers like Tesla Wall Connectors?

+

Yes. Sestito Electrical installs Tesla Wall Connectors, universal NEMA 14-50 outlets, and hardwired Level 2 EV chargers from every major brand. Every installation includes a proper load calculation, a dedicated circuit sized for the charger, and permit coordination with the local inspector.

How do I get a free estimate?

+

Call Stefano directly at 617-529-1019 or email Stefano.sestito@yahoo.com. Every estimate starts with a free on-site walkthrough where Stefano reviews the scope, discusses options honestly, and then delivers an itemized written estimate within a few days. Response time is one business day or less.

Does Sestito Electrical work on luxury custom homes?

+

Luxury residential is the core of the business. Stefano specializes in high-end homes across the North Shore, handling everything from full new-construction wiring to retrofit work in historic and custom homes. Every installation is designed for both code compliance and the aesthetic detail luxury homes demand.

What makes Sestito Electrical different from other North Shore electricians?

+

Three things. Stefano is union-trained through IBEW Local 103 with 20 years of experience on major commercial projects including Mass General Brigham hospitals and Patriot Place at Gillette Stadium. He is personally on site for every job instead of dispatching crews. And every estimate is itemized and transparent with permit and inspection costs included up front. No surprises.
